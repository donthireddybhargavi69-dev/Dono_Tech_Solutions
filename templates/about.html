Our Journey

2014

Founding of DONO CONSULTANCY

DONO CONSULTANCY was established in 2014 as a boutique consultancy firm, operating from a single office & It is a dedicated telecalling services for academic institutions, focusing on student admissions, and educational support communication. This initiative marked a strategic shift into the education support sector, the company laid the foundation for its future expansion into BPO and IT services.

2023

Incorporation of DONO BPO & IT SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD.

Officially registered as a private limited company, DONO BPO & IT SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. specializes in Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), IT services, and business support operations. The company primarily focuses on student admissions through tele-calling, providing targeted outreach and personalized assistance to educational institutions. Additionally, DONO is backed by a skilled digital marketing team that enhances brand visibility and lead generation through strategic online campaigns, making it a comprehensive partner for academic outreach and marketing solutions.

2025

Launch of DONOTECH SOLUTIONS

Evolving to meet market demands, DONOTECH Solutions was launched with a focused mission: bridging the skills gap between academia and industry through targeted training programs and professional mentorship initiatives.
